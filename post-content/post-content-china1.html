Zhangjiajie Wonders in a Week: Peaks, Canyons, and Ancient Charms

  19 December 2015

Embarked on a week-long adventure in Zhangjiajie, China, starting with the iconic peaks of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, a surreal landscape straight out of 'Avatar.' Explored Tianzi Mountain for panoramic views, conquered Tianmen Cave, and dared the Glass Skywalk. Mid-week brought thrills at Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon's Glass Bridge, balanced with the tranquility of Zhangjiajie Old Courtyard. Cultural immersion followed in the ancient charm of Fenghuang Ancient Town, strolling along the Phoenix Ancient City Wall. The week concluded with the Tianmen Fox Fairy Show, a mesmerizing performance against the backdrop of Tianmen Mountain.
