Embracing Nature's Palette: A 3-Day Retreat in Cameron Highlands

  6 August 2023

Embarked on a blissful 3-day journey through the enchanting landscapes of Cameron Highlands, a verdant paradise in Malaysia. Day one unfolded at the Green View Garden, where vibrant blooms painted a colorful tapestry, setting the tone for the trip. A visit to Cactus Point showcased the diversity of flora, with quirky cactus varieties stealing the spotlight. Day two led me to the mystical Mossy Forest, where ancient trees and misty trails transported me to a fairytale realm. The day reached its peak with a soul-soothing lunch at Boh Tea Plantation, surrounded by rolling hills of emerald green. Cameron Centrum and Kea Farm provided glimpses into local life, while Zoomania de Butterfly Farm offered a kaleidoscope of winged wonders.
